About this corpus

Lifecycle statuses

proposed
ADR is open for the objection window. Not yet binding.
ratified
Binding in its declared scope. Imposition prevented by recorded-objection discipline.
superseded
Replaced by a later ADR. See the superseded_by field.
withdrawn
Retracted before ratification.
CARRIED
Ratified at the authoring repo; proposed at platform scope. Cross-repo authority not yet conferred.

SCOPED-binding model

An ADR may carry both an origin_status (e.g., ratified) and a platform_status (e.g., proposed) when it has been ratified within the authoring repository but its authority has not yet been extended across all platform repos.

The adr/INDEX.yaml supports filtering by platform_status: ratified to identify decisions that are universally binding.
